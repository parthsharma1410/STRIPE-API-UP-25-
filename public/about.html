ABOUT

We are a music enthusiast group from the city of Bareilly (UP) and are on the mission to spread peace through music. Every band member is immensely talented in his field of instrument.

We have Ishan on the keyboard, Chinmay handling the vocals, Parth involved in rap and guitar, Kabir with the drums and Sparsh with vocals. We also have a dancing monkey named Aakar.
